Overview

Professional collaboration dashboard

This CRM module does not rebuild chat from scratch. Instead, it gives your admin panel a beautiful launch surface that opens the actual Microsoft Teams web app, where admins can create teams, add channels, manage members, schedule meetings, share files, and use OneDrive storage.

Quick Tips

Best demo flow

  • Sign in with your Microsoft 365 account before launching Teams.
  • Use Teams for channels, meetings, calls, and enterprise chat.
  • Use OneDrive for document uploads, share links, and storage access.
